Features

Everything you need
to prepare

📋
Real exam mode

30 questions, 30-minute countdown — exactly like the actual citizenship test. The timer turns orange at 5 minutes and red at 1 minute. At the end you'll see your overall score as well as a breakdown by each topic: History, Governance, Equality and Labor Law.

🎓
Training mode

Get instant explanations after every answer. Configure question count freely. No time pressure — learn at your own pace.

🌍
8 hint languages

Quiz questions in Finnish or Swedish. Read hints and explanations in your native language — 8 options so nothing gets lost in translation.

📚
120+ questions

Over 120 questions covering all topics of the Finnish citizenship exam, each with a detailed explanation. As new methodological materials from the Finnish Immigration Service are released, the question bank will be updated accordingly.

How it works

Three steps to
exam-ready

1
Choose your mode

Pick Training to learn with explanations, or Real to simulate the actual 30-question exam under timed conditions.

2
Answer questions

Select your question language and the language for hints. Work through topics in any order you like.

3
Review & retry

See your score after every session. Review each question with the correct answer and retry until you feel confident.

About

Built by someone
who needed it

Voluka is a sole proprietorship registered in Helsinki, Finland. We build mobile applications focused on education, knowledge, and personal development.

The idea to create application IknowFinland was born from a simple personal need: a reliable, multilingual tool for people preparing for the Finnish citizenship test. We built what we needed — and made it available to everyone.

Following the successful launch of our integration knowledge apps — IknowFinland, IknowGermany, IknowFrance and beyond — our next ambition is to expand into entertainment and leisure. We believe the same care and craft that makes a great study tool can make a great experience app.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

IknowFinland ("the App") is developed by Voluka. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your quiz settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google may use collected data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads depending on your consent choices.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Since the App does not directly collect personal data, requests regarding advertising data should be directed to Google.

You can opt out of personalised advertising on your device:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

Content & Sources

The quiz questions and explanations included in our applications are based solely on publicly available materials, including:

  • Official integration course study guides and workbooks published by government and public institutions
  • Publicly available civic knowledge handbooks and citizenship exam preparation materials
  • Open educational resources and reference publications in the public domain

Voluka does not claim ownership of any factual civic or historical content. All questions reflect publicly available knowledge intended to help users prepare for official citizenship examinations. If you believe any content requires clarification or correction, please contact us.
